For athletes who'd rather lift than log.

LiftHit is a focused weight calculator for powerlifters and strength athletes. Enter your one-rep maxes for the big lifts, and LiftHit instantly calculates every percentage-based working set. Supports kg and lbs. Data stays on your device — no account required.

Support

Questions, bug reports, or feature ideas? Email lifthit@sekava.dev — every message is read.

Where is my data stored?
On your device, with optional sync through your personal iCloud account. The developer never sees it. No account is needed to use LiftHit.
Which units do you support?
Both kilograms and pounds. You can also set the smallest plate available in the settings for precise bar load calculation.
What exercises are supported?
LiftHit comes with built-in support for the big three: bench, deadlift, and squat. And military press, because — shoulders. But you can add your own custom exercises too.
Still need help?
